Governance

Clear governance for collaborative health AI.

CHOIR is developing a governance model that supports transparency, accountability, collaboration, and responsible AI research across institutions.

Governance Structure

Built around leadership, working groups, and member participation.

Steering Committee

Provides strategic oversight, reviews priorities, supports accountability, and guides the overall direction of CHOIR.

Working Groups

Lead focused initiatives in areas such as federated learning, knowledge translation, common data models, and training.

Member Community

Participates in programs, proposes initiatives, contributes expertise, and helps shape CHOIR priorities.

Terms of Reference

A formal operating framework is being developed.

01Define roles and responsibilities
02Establish working group structure
03Clarify membership expectations
04Set meeting and decision-making processes
05Support transparency, EDI, and conflict management

Governance Principles

Designed for trust and responsible collaboration.

Transparency

Clear processes, expectations, and communication across the alliance.

Accountability

Defined roles, responsibilities, and oversight for CHOIR activities.

Equity & Inclusion

Participation across institutions, disciplines, geographies, and career stages.

Responsible AI

Privacy, governance, validation, and real-world impact built into collaboration.

Help Shape CHOIR

Governance will evolve with the alliance.
