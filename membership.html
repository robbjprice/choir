Membership

A collaborative alliance built by its members.

CHOIR welcomes researchers, clinicians, health systems, technical experts, trainees, industry collaborators, and strategic partners who share a commitment to advancing trustworthy health AI.

Membership Categories

Multiple ways to participate.

Research Members

Academic investigators, research teams, and research institutes seeking collaborative opportunities and shared infrastructure.

Health System Members

Hospitals, health authorities, clinics, and operational teams interested in AI-enabled improvement and innovation.

Technical Members

Data scientists, engineers, software developers, and AI specialists supporting collaborative projects.

Trainees

Students, fellows, and early-career researchers building skills and networks.

Industry Partners

Organizations interested in collaborative development and validation initiatives.

Strategic Partners

Government, non-profit, and ecosystem organizations aligned with CHOIR's mission.

International Collaborators

Global partners advancing privacy-preserving health AI research.
