Join CHOIR

Become part of Canada's collaborative health AI community.

CHOIR brings together researchers, clinicians, health systems, technical experts, trainees, industry partners, and innovation leaders who share a commitment to advancing trustworthy and impactful health AI.

Who Should Join?

CHOIR is designed for a diverse health AI ecosystem.

Researchers

Collaborate across institutions, access expertise, and strengthen grant applications.

Health Systems

Explore, validate, and implement AI solutions that address real-world challenges.

Technical Experts

Contribute data science, engineering, infrastructure, and AI expertise.

Trainees

Build networks, gain mentorship, and participate in collaborative projects.

Industry Partners

Engage in collaborative innovation, validation, and implementation opportunities.

Strategic Partners

Support ecosystem growth through policy, funding, and partnership initiatives.

International Collaborators

Join a growing global network focused on privacy-preserving health AI research.
