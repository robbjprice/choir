About CHOIR

A University of Calgary-led alliance for collaborative health AI.

CHOIR brings together researchers, clinicians, health systems, technical experts, trainees, and innovation partners to advance privacy-preserving AI research and implementation.

Our Story

Built for collaboration across fragmented health data systems.

CHOIR was created in response to a clear national challenge: health data are distributed across institutions, jurisdictions, and governance environments. Rather than requiring sensitive data to move, CHOIR supports collaboration through shared expertise, federated infrastructure, working groups, and coordinated research services.

Mission & Vision

Connecting people, infrastructure, and innovation.

Mission

To enable collaborative, trustworthy, and impactful health AI research by connecting expertise, infrastructure, and innovation across institutions and jurisdictions.

Vision

A future where collaborative health AI improves health outcomes by enabling researchers and organizations to work together securely, efficiently, and at scale.

Purpose

To create a durable alliance and shared innovation core that helps health AI move from research ideas to validated, implemented, and continuously improving tools.

Why CHOIR?
