What is CHOIR?

A research alliance built for distributed, privacy-preserving health AI.

CHOIR helps institutions collaborate without centralizing sensitive patient data. Through federated learning, shared governance, working groups, and member services, CHOIR supports multi-site research, grant development, AI implementation, and learning health system innovation.

Why CHOIR?

Canada’s health AI potential is limited by fragmented data and disconnected infrastructure.

Data Silos

Health data are governed locally and often cannot be centralized across institutions.

Access Barriers

Advanced AI infrastructure and expertise are not equally available to every research team.

Static Models

AI models can become outdated as patient populations, workflows, and clinical practices evolve.

How CHOIR Works

Collaboration without moving patient-level data.

CHOIR supports federated AI workflows where data remain within local institutional environments. Models are trained locally, shared insights are aggregated securely, and validated tools can be refined across multiple sites.

Research Programs

Four areas of national-scale health AI collaboration.

Multimodal AI

Population-scale models using clinical, imaging, text, and health system data.

Precision Medicine

Adaptive risk prediction models that evolve with changing patient populations.

System Optimization

Forecasting and planning tools for resource use, referral demand, and system sustainability.

Learning Health Systems

Clinical implementation, monitoring, updating, and real-world evaluation of AI tools.

Featured Use Case

KDpredict shows what CHOIR is designed to support.

KDpredict is an example of AI moving from research toward clinical decision support. CHOIR is designed to help more tools follow that path: from model development, to validation, to implementation, to continuous learning.
